Sacramento deserve to know what is in the budget and how it is made.

Open Budget: Sacramento takes current and past budget data published by the City of Sacramento and transforms them into accessible visualizations. Our aim is to enable community members to examine their city's budget for themselves.

We have a suite of data viewing tools to help you understand Sacramento's budget.

Overview

Get a bird's eye view of the big picture for a single year: where money comes from, and where it goes.

Detail

Drill down into detailed spending and revenue data for each department and fund for a single year.

Custom Queries

Explore the details of the City of Sacramento's adopted 2018 budget.

Open Budget: Sacramento is an open-source project by Code for Sacramento that seeks to help residents understand Sacramento's spending and budget process better. This application is a clone of Open Budget: Oakland, from OpenOakland.
